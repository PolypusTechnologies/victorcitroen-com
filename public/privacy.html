03Cookies & consent

By default this site sets no cookies. Vercel Web Analytics is cookieless and runs without consent. Google Analytics only runs if you click "Accept analytics" in the cookie banner — until then it is blocked via Google Consent Mode (analytics storage denied). Your choice is stored locally in your browser; you can clear it any time via your browser's site-data settings, which will show the banner again.

If you previously accepted and want to opt out, clear this site's data in your browser settings, or decline when the banner reappears. You can also block analytics cookies in your browser.

The site is hosted on Vercel. Like any web host, Vercel processes standard server request data (e.g. IP address) transiently to deliver the site and for security. See Vercel's privacy documentation for details.
