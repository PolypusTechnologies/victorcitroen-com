SAGE Jr.

A GDPR-compliant AI support system for a Dutch managed-service provider. It ingests support tickets, redacts personal data locally, and drafts responses through Claude with confidence-based routing — auto, draft-for-review, or escalate.

I'm delivering it as Product Owner — sole owner of the architecture and GDPR design — driving an AI-assisted build end to end across an 11-phase roadmap.

  • SSO, secrets management, encrypted at rest
  • Shadow-mode pilot before any automated action
